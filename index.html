🛠️ Microscope not showing up?
  1. Check that the microscope is switched on and firmly connected.
  2. Choose Allow when the browser asks to use the camera.
  3. Open the camera menu above and choose the USB or microscope camera.
  4. If it needs more power, try a powered USB hub.
  5. Close other apps that may already be using the microscope.

This page processes video on your device. It does not upload your camera feed.
